Setting the record straight

What document intelligence
is not.

There is a great deal of noise in this market. We prefer directness.

Misconception 01
"AI document processing means my data trains someone else's model"
Every processing pipeline Rixen deploys operates under contractual Zero-Data Retention. Your documents — bordereaux, claims files, financial schedules — are processed and immediately purged. They never enter a public model. Full audit trail provided.
Misconception 02
"Automation requires replacing our core system infrastructure"
We operate on top of what you already have. Rixen connects to your existing ERP, core insurance platform, or data repository. No rip-and-replace. No vendor lock-in. Your system of record stays unchanged.
Misconception 03
"A black-box AI pipeline cannot meet our compliance requirements"
Rixen's architecture is built for regulated environments. Every data transformation is logged, signed, and traceable. Human-in-the-loop review is part of the design — not an afterthought. Deliverables include full audit documentation.
